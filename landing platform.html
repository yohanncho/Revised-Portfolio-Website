Landing Platform

An RC trimaran driven by a motorized dual propeller system and built out of pinewood. All three pontoons are hollowed and joined with wooden rods. The Arduino circuit board is connected to speed controllers, motors and a receiver. This device was designed in a project course where my team was tasked with building a barge to carry a 30-centimeter rocket without dropping it into the water. The idea for this project was developed to replicate the SpaceX landing platforms used in their rocket launches from the ocean.

To maximize efficiency, we conducted many simulations to test battery power outputs, drag coefficients and hull stability. We finished as one of the top maneuvering devices in the class as we raced the devices in a competition style format and even got interviewed by Breakfast Television!

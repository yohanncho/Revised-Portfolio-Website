Robotic Arm

The application was developed using MIT App Inventor and can be programmed to repeatedly perform a specific sequence of motions. This personal project involved mostly 3D printed components, 5 load bearing servo motors, an HC-05 Bluetooth module and many fasteners.

Rail Rider

A motorized rail vehicle able to maneuver around a track while pulling cargo and scaling inclines. The chassis is 3D printed with PLA filament and structurally supported with aluminum supports. The gear train has a 1:24 ratio and the battery is mounted on the underside of the chassis in order to lower the center of gravity. The front wheels have a swivelling functionality to minimize chances of derailment while conforming to sharp bends of track.

Electromagnetic Levitation

A battery powered electromagnetic device used to levitate a magnetic object. The aluminum foundation was fabricated utilizing a lathe, mill and various other machinery. The circuit board is fully soldered and connected to an induction coil. The intensity of pull is toggled by turning the offset and gain located at the back of the circuit board. When powered on, this device will lift the object in midair as vertical forces balance out.

Landing Platform

An RC trimaran driven by a motorized dual propeller system and built out of pinewood. All three pontoons are hollowed and joined with wooden rods. The Arduino circuit board is connected to speed controllers, motors and a receiver. This device was designed in a project course where my team was tasked with building a barge to carry a 30-centimeter rocket without dropping it into the water.
